Chewhys Comics

Introduction

Chewhys Comics is a non-profit organization totally run by students in Shanghai Pinghe School. It is dedicated to sharing philosophy knowledge. Led by students who have a passion for philosophy, the organization creates innovative comics to people having little knowledge in philosophy. The aim of the organization is to enhance people's interest in philosophy and promote a vibrant philosophy atmosphere within the society.
