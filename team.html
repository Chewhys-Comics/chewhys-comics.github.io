Our Team

Chewhys Comics is a non-profit organization totally run by students in Shanghai Pinghe School. It is dedicated to sharing philosophy knowledge. Led by students who have a passion for philosophy, the organization creates innovative comics to people having little knowledge in philosophy. The aim of the organization is to enhance people's interest in philosophy and promote a vibrant philosophy atmosphere within the society. Our team members are listed below:

Founder: Chloe Ke
Current Director: Jiachen Shi
Core Members: Meiheng Li, Muriel Cao, Sade Wang, Suni Yao

Donate to Our Organization

Our work are totally voluntary and with no rewards. By contributing to the Chewhys Comics, you will help us to provide better quality instruction for students.

We would be grateful for any amount of contribution. With your support the Chewhys Comics will continue to develop!
